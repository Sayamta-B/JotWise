JotWise is a web-based platform designed for users to discover, review, and engage with their favorite series through short and sweet reviews. It is aimed to provide users with short jots about the series to get a basic idea of the series. It has user-friendly interface which allows visitors to browse various series, add them to their favorites and share their thoughts through comments. Admins have the capability to manage users and series, ensuring smooth content management and an organized browsing experience. The platform emphasizes simplicity, making it easy for users to interact with content through basic CRUD (Create, Read, Update, Delete) operations.
